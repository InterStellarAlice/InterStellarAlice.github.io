费曼图的基本概念

前面得到了零温单粒子格林函数在相互作用绘景下的表达式:
$$
\hspace{0.1em}\mathrm{i}\hspace{0.1em}G_{\alpha \beta}\left(x ; {x}^{\prime}\right)=\frac{\left\langle\Phi^{0}\left|\hat{T}\left[\hat{\psi}^{}{I \alpha}(x) \hat{\psi}{I \beta}^{\dagger}\left({x}^{\prime}\right) \hat{S}\right]\right| \Phi^{0}\right\rangle}{\left\langle\Phi^{0}\right|\hat{S}\left| \Phi^{0}\right\rangle},

\begin{aligned}
\hat{S} &\equiv\hat{T} \exp \left[-\hspace{0.1em}\mathrm{i}\hspace{0.1em}\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \mathrm{d} t \hat{V}{I}(t)\right] \
&=\mathbb{1}-\hspace{0.1em}\mathrm{i}\hspace{0.1em}\int{-\infty}^{\infty} \mathrm{d} t \hat{V}{I}(t)+\frac{(-\mathrm{i})^{2}}{2 !} \int{-\infty}^{\infty} \mathrm{d} t_{1} \int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \mathrm{d} t_{2} \hat{T}\left[\hat{V}{I}(t{1}) \hat{V}{I}(t{2})\right]+\cdots.
\end{aligned}

\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} \mathrm{d} t \hat{V}{I}(t)=\frac{1}{2} \sum{\alpha ,\beta} \iint \mathrm{d} x \mathrm{d} x^{\prime} \hat{\psi}{I \alpha}^{\dagger}(x) \hat{\psi}{I \beta}^{\dagger}(x^{\prime}) \hat U(x; x^{\prime}) \hat{\psi}^{}{I \beta}(x^{\prime}) \hat{\psi}^{}{I \alpha}(x) ,
